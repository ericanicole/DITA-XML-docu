How to Divide Numbers

Here you will find instructions about using the division feature of the JPS Calculator app.

To successfully divide numbers, you must know division.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Choose the obelus/divide (รท) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal (=) button.

The result from the division is displayed.

You want to divide 6 by 3. The equation will look like 6รท3=2. You enter 3. Press divide. Enter 2. Press equals.

The result 2 appears on the screen.
